About Aurevix

A technology partner built on precision, people, and performance.

Aurevix Infosolutions Inc. is a U.S.-based IT consulting, staffing, and technology marketing firm headquartered in Dallas, Texas. We work alongside enterprises, mid-market companies, and government agencies to solve real technology challenges — from cloud modernization and cybersecurity to sourcing the specialized talent that makes transformation possible.

Our approach blends deep technical expertise with a genuinely consultative process. Every engagement starts with understanding your business outcomes first, then architecting the people, platforms, and processes to get there — reliably, securely, and on schedule.
